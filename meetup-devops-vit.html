Event Recap

"Many ideas grow better when transplanted into another mind than the one where they sprang up."
-- Oliver Wendell Holmes

With this initiative to share the knowledge again with other sophomore and greenhorns of university, GirlScript Jaipur chapter organized its second meetup at Vivekananda Institute of technology with great success.
The main motto of this meetup was to deliver some beginners knowledge of DevOps so that young minds can get a little glimpse of how corporate works. We collaborated with Computer Society for Technocrats (CST) club of Vivekananda Institute of Technology for this meetup. The club goes for making a workplace for understudies wherein they can trade their insight and thoughts to learn and help one another. Varshit conveyed decent insights about DevOps to the students which gave knowledge about how to go ahead to pursue more knowledge in DevOps.
